United Nations
Development Goals

The 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015, provides a shared blueprint for peace and prosperity for people and the planet, now and into the future.

The Sustainable Development Goals are a set of 17 goals made by the United Nations in 2015. These goals help address global challenges and aim to inspire action toward a cleaner and greener world!
